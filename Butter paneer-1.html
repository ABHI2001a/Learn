Paneer makhani (also called as butter paneer) is a slightly sweet creamy dish of paneer,originating from indian subcontinent,in which the gravy is prepared usually with butter,tomatoes,cashews or cream.

How to cook butter paneer

INGREDIENTS

  • 250 grams paneer(cut into rectangles)
  • 6 Tomatoes
  • 15 kaju(cashew nuts)
  • garlic cloves(crushed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt for taste
  • Red chilli pwder
  • 2 tbsp fenugreek leaves
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Cream (optional)
  • 2-3 tbsp milk

    • METHOD

  1. Add 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Once it melts ,add garlic,cinnamon and cashews.🥣
  2. Fry , till the colour changes. Then add tomatoes.🍅
  3. Saute till the the tomatoes become soft and add chilli powder and salt .🥗
  4. Saute for 2-3 minutes and then grind to it into a fine paste.🥢
  5. Take another pan - Add 1 tbsp butter and grill the paneer pieces till golden brown on both sides.Remove the paneer pieces.🍲
  6. In the same pan add the tomato-cashew paste .Let it simmer for 3-4 mintues.🥄
  7. Now add kasoori methi and sugar. Let it simmer for 1 mintue.🥛
  8. Add milk ,paneer pieces and top it with cream if desired.🍳
  9. Serve hot.🥘
